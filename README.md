# Business Operations Performance Analysis – Kitengela Fresh Mart

## Project Overview

This project analyzes the operational performance of Kitengela Fresh Mart, a retail business operating in Kitengela, Kenya. The objective is to evaluate sales performance, operational expenses, inventory management, and employee productivity in order to generate actionable business insights and support decision-making.

The project was developed using Microsoft Excel and includes data cleaning, KPI calculations, pivot table analysis, and an interactive business dashboard.

---

## Business Problem

Retail businesses generate large volumes of operational data daily. However, without proper analysis, management may struggle to:

* Monitor profitability
* Control operational expenses
* Track inventory levels
* Evaluate employee productivity
* Identify top-performing products and categories

This project addresses these challenges by transforming raw operational data into meaningful business insights.

## Key Insights

* Generated over KES 1.29 million in total revenue.
* Achieved a gross profit of approximately KES 355,270.
* Maintained positive profitability throughout the analysis period.
* Identified product categories contributing the highest revenue.
* Monitored stock levels to prevent inventory shortages.
* Evaluated employee productivity using attendance and task completion metrics.

---

## Business Recommendations

1. Increase stock levels for high-performing products.
2. Monitor and optimize major expense categories.
3. Reward high-performing employees to improve motivation.
4. Use sales trends to improve inventory planning.
5. Implement regular KPI monitoring for better decision-making.
